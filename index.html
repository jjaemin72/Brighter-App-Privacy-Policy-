Privacy Policy for Brighter

Last Updated: December 06, 2024

Welcome to Brighter! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using Brighter, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Brighter processes the following types of data to provide its services:

  1. Face Data: Images of your face are processed to provide customized skincare analysis.
    • Why we collect this data: To generate personalized skincare recommendations and ratings tailored to your unique skin needs.
    • What happens to this data: Face data is analyzed locally on your device or temporarily during processing but is never stored, shared, or used for any purpose other than providing analysis.
  2. Other Data: Information you voluntarily provide, such as your skincare preferences or usage patterns, may be used to enhance your experience within the app.
    • What happens to this data: This data is processed locally and is not shared with third parties.

Data Storage and Sharing

Brighter is designed with your privacy in mind.

  • We do not store any personal data, including face data or analysis results, beyond the duration of your active session.
  • Your face data is processed solely for the purpose of providing immediate feedback and is not retained on our servers or devices.
  • We do not share your data with any third parties, advertisers, or external entities.

Your Consent

By using Brighter, you consent to the processing of face data and other information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the app.

Data Security

Brighter employs advanced security measures to ensure that any data processed during your session is protected. While we do not store your data, we take every precaution to ensure secure transmission and analysis.

Your Rights

Because we do not store your personal data, there is no stored information to access, modify, or delete. However, you have the right to withdraw consent to the processing of your data at any time by ceasing to use the app.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app, and the updated policy will take effect immediately. Please review the Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

Thank you for trusting Brighter. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and delivering the best personalized skincare experience possible.
